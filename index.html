Scene Select
Left click on a scene to start it.
Right click on a scene to favorite it.
Click the left or right arrows in the top right to change pages.
Click on the page number, type a new page number in to it and press enter to jump to that page.
Viewer
Click or press Space to advance the scene.
Press A or the Auto button at the bottom to enter auto mode.
Hold Left control or press the skip button to enter skip mode.
Press Esc or the Exit button to end the scene.
Search
Press the search button to open the search menu.
Character can be called by multiple names e.g. "Igawa Asagi", "Asagi Igawa" and "Asagi".
Artist can be called by multiple names e.g. "Kagami Hirotaka", "Hirotaka Kagami" and "Kagami".
Enter tags in to the Female, Male and Misc inputs
Tick match all to make the scenes require all of the tags you entered.
Seperate Names Tags and Artists with a comma, e.g. "Asagi", "Sakura"
Tick exclusive characters to only find scenes with characters unique to TABA and Taimanin RPGX.
Tick Main series characters to only find scenes with characters that are featured in other games.
Tick favorites only to only find scenes that are in your favorites.
Tick English only to only find scenes with an English translation.
Press Cancel to go back to the previous menu.
Press Reset to set scene select back to default.
Press Search to start a search, if no results are found you will get an error message.
